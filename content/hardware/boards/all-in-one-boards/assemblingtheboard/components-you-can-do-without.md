Depending on what you are planning to do with the boards, you can save a bit of money by skipping some of the individual components when you order from JLCPCB. When ordering from JLCPCB the minimum quantity is 5 boards. If you are planning on selling the extra boards you might reconsider skipping some of the individual components.


As of May 2026, these are the parts and the prices that you don't have to order if you will not be using them. The price is for 5 boards. 

If you are not using a Danfoss hydraulic valve system, you don't need: U3 (3 USD), H22 (0.10 USD), R10 (0.01 USD)

If you are not using a steering wheel that supports CANBUS, you don't need U17 (6 USD) or D5 (1.40 USD). As a reminder, the KEYA steering option uses CANBUS for communication!
